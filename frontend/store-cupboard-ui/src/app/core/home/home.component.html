Welcome to Pantry!

Tracking the groceries in your fridge, freezer, and pantry is simple with Pantry!

It provides shopping list capabilities, as well as allowing you to scan barcodes on individual products for easier entry.

Plus, if you are at the grocery store and cannot remember if you need to replace a certain product, the barcode scanner allows the app to notify you if you are running low and need to purchase. Choose a specific storage area for each item and you will be able to look in each location for what is ready for use.
